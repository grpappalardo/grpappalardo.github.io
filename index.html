About Me

Hi! I'm a GIS Intern at Ballotpedia, "the encyclopedia of American politics".

I studied Geographic Information Science at Clark University, concentrating in Remote Sensing and did tech support and software testing for Clark Labs, a program out of the Graduate School of Geography at Clark which develops the TerrSet Geospatial Monitoring and Modeling System, first released in 1987 and used in over 180 countries.

Outside of academia, I am an avid home cook and enjoy music, writing, and reading (from Russian literature to fantasy/sci-fi).
